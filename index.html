MY FIRST CONSOLE WEBSITE

Visual code studio is an intergrated development environment by microsoft that aids coders in making websites.



The console.log() method writes a message to the console.
The console is useful for testing purposes.
To open the console screen, you right click and click on inspect (U), and then click on "console"
You can also press the function key (Fn) + F12 and then click on console to open the console screen.
